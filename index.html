Directions From Dad

How To Play

You will be given a starting cardinal direction. You will then be given a list of left or right turns. All you need to do is tell us which direction you are facing after following the turns. Be Careful though, wrong answers will cost you time!

Example:

North
Left Right Right

In this example you would first turn left and be facing West. Then after turning right you would be facing North again. After your second right turn you would be facing East. This is the correct answer!

North
